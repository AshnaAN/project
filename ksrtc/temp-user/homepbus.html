Hiring

KSRTC has launched a platform for hiring buses using which you can hire a bus as per your requirement.Rent a KSRTC bus for your group of friends, family or colleagues. You can rent any type of bus (Ac/Non Ac) and reach your destination in a jiffy.Have Travel Plans? Just Rent a Bus! You can rent a bus for one-way and return trips as well as for local or outstation journeys. Buses are more economical than renting multiple cars, and provide you comfort and ease during travel.

How it works:

  1. You give us your trip details.
  2. Select the bus of your choice.
  3. We will arrange the bus on specified date.

    4. Voila! Go on your trip
